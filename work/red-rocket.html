Red Rocket

Red Rocket, Web Design & Digital Campaign

Red Rocket ($REDRKT) is a meme-powered Solana token built around Captain Chuck Borris and his crew of Pawsmonauts — an ever-expanding universe of characters, gaming experiences, and collectible cosmic lore. The brand lives at the intersection of Web3 culture, retro gaming, and community-first storytelling.

Our work covered the full digital presence: site architecture, visual design, and art direction — translating the brand's playful-yet-ambitious identity into a fast, immersive web experience. From the hero splash and tokenomics sections to the Red Rocket League game teaser and MoonDrops loyalty system, every screen was designed to make holders feel like they were already aboard the ship.
