Fat Goblin Games Brand Identity

Fat Goblin Games lives at the intersection of outsider art and tabletop culture — raw, expressive, and unapologetically itself. We built a brand identity that leans into that energy: bold type, street-influenced illustration, and a visual language that feels like it was spraypainted onto a dungeon wall.

The result is a system designed to stand out in a crowded genre — on shelves, on screens, and in the hands of the players who love it.
